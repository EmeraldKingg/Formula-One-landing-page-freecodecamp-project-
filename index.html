2025 Formula 1 Teams

The 2025 Formula 1 season features ten teams on the starting grid: McLaren, Ferrari, Mercedes, Red Bull Racing, Williams, Racing Bulls, Aston Martin, Haas F1 Team, Kick Sauber, and Alpine.

2025 Formula 1 Drivers

As of the 2025 Formula 1 season, the driver standings are led by Lando Norris and Oscar Piastri of McLaren, who are tied at the top of the championship with 357 and 356 points respectively. Max Verstappen of Red Bull Racing follows in third place with 321 points. The current drivers in the 2025 Formula 1 grid include notable names such as George Russell, Charles Leclerc, Lewis Hamilton, and Kimi Antonelli, among others.

Grand Prix Races

Formula One (F1) is the highest class of worldwide racing for open-wheel, single-seater formula racing cars sanctioned by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). In 2025, there is a total of 24 races, all races are held in different countries around the globe.
